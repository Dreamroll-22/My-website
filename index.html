What is Computer System Servicing and Programming?

Computer System Servicing (CSS) refers to the safety and assist activities carried out on Computer structures to make certain their terrific functioning. CSS focuses on overall maintenance of computer systems, including the installation, repair, and troubleshooting of hardware and software. CSS also involves keeping peripherals in good condition, setting up networks, and addressing system failures or issues.


CSS play a vital role in keeping computer systems and networks in optimal working condition. Their responsibilities includes the following:

Installing

Installing operating system, software, and peripheral devices.

Maintaining

Ensuring that all computer systems and devices are operational and updated.

Repairing

Fixing hardware and software problems to restore normal system functions.

Networking

Setting up, configuring, and maintaining local area networks (LANs) and other types of connections.

Troubleshooting

Diagnosing and fixing system issues effectively.
